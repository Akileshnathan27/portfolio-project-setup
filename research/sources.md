## Selection Criteria

These 10 experts were chosen using the following filter:

1. **They run something** — an agency, a SaaS company, a marketing team,
   or a community. No pure educators.
2. **They use LinkedIn themselves** — their own profiles are proof of
   the strategies they teach.
3. **They publish systems, not just opinions** — frameworks, reports,
   or documented playbooks exist for each person.
4. **They are cited by other practitioners** — cross-referenced across
   multiple industry lists and peer recommendations.
