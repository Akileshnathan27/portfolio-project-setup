Our 4th annual Spryng conference is done and here's what it taught me about B2B sales:



1\. It's a bunch of work leading up to it, but then it's so fun when it all comes together.

2\. People make the event. This hasn't changed.

3\. Creating a welcoming and chill atmosphere at the event is not an accident, you don't see it at most hotel-based events. We optimize for fun, and fun is a serious business.

4\. Selling tickets is the hardest part of the conference every year. Logistics are easy.

5\. Conferences are a great way to see friends you don't run into otherwise. As a conference organizer, you can also invite your friends. Perks.

6\. Don't underestimate the quality of food. People will notice if it's good.

7\. Events are marketing and relationship building. You can spend the money on LinkedIn ads, or you can create a memorable experience.
