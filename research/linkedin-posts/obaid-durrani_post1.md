"Until we have confidence knowing we're hitting our ICP on Facebook, I'm not spending more ad dollars there"



^^ that's been me since about 2022 as I watched Facebook's targeting + segmentation functionality get worse and worse as each quarter passed



At one point, it was my go-to social media platform to get in front of our ICP - we could target the right people, hit them at incredibly affordable CPMs, and reap the benefits as they entered our pipeline over time



Fast forward recent times, and I have no idea if someone gets pulled into a 1% lookalike because they match an uploaded list based on things like their occupation + industry



...or if they're matching because some members of the list also happen to like cats, so the lookalike honed in on anyone who has a very strong affinity toward cat videos



But that has \*finally\* changed



For years I've been saying match rates on platforms like Facebook and Google are garbage because people sign in + use those using their PERSONAL email address, NOT their work email address



AKA, while we marketers have a TON of information about our ICP and the people who make it up, the constraint we face is that most platforms are limited to work email matching



...until now



Today Clay officially released their ads functionality I'd been playing around with in beta + was blown away by



👋 No more garbage match rates thanks to the work + personal email waterfall combo

👋 No more building out individual inclusion + exclusion lists in each platform thanks to audience building that actually makes sense in 2026

👋 No more hoping you pull in the right contacts based on their job title or function thanks to the ability to see exactly \*who\* in your ICP does/doesn't make it into the list
