Are you putting out bangers in B2B? 



My guest this week is Erin May 🎙️ (CMO at User Interviews)



She joined the company as the full-time first marketer, started with a two-page website and zero recurring revenue and helped them grow past $20M+ ARR over eight years. 



Erin breaks down how she picked one niche audience and went all in, why she led with ungated content, and how she built a quarterly operating rhythm around "Bangers" (i love this term for it) that get the entire company involved.



We also get into podcasting ROI, what she'd do differently with AI-driven search today, and why she thinks the brand vs. demand debate is just noise.
